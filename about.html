About Me

Who I Am

I grew up in Cambridge, Massachusetts, and am currently a student at MIT. In high school, I did math competitions, and Public Forum debate, both relatively competitively. Now, I'm a student at MIT, working on AI control through the Cambridge Boston Alignment Initiative Summer Research Fellowship. I'll be taking a gap semester this spring to attend Constellation's Astra Fellowship for Redwood Research's empirical stream. I'm a member of Impact@MIT (MIT's EA Club), and MIT AI Alignment (MAIA).
